Gary Vaynerchuk

Serial Entrepreneur & Investor

5x New York Times Best Selling Author

Chairman of Vayner-X

CEO of VaynerMedia

Timeline

  • 1975 - born in Babruysk, Belarus
  • 1978 - immigrates to United States
  • 1998 - after graduating from college in 1998, Vaynerchuk took charge of his father's liquor store, Shopper's Discount Liquors.
  • 2006 - Vaynerchuk hosted a video blog on YouTube called Wine Library TV featuring wine reviews, tastings, and wine advice
  • 2009 - in 2009, Vaynerchuk, along with his brother AJ Vaynerchuk, founded VaynerMedia, a social mediaâ€“focused digital agency
  • 2010 - in 2010, Vaynerchuk launched Wine & Web on Sirius XM satellite radio. The show's programming paired new wine tastings in a "Wine of the Week" segment with coverage of gadgets, trends and startups in its "Web of the Week" segment
  • 2014 - in 2014, Vaynerchuk launched The #AskGaryVee Show on YouTube in which he responds to questions from Twitter and Instagram
  • 2015 - DailyVee is a daily, video-documentary series on YouTube hosted by Vaynerchuk. Started in 2015, he records interviews with other businessmen and broadcasts investor meetings and strategy sessions at VaynerMedia
  • 2015 - in 2015 Vaynerchuk was named to Crain's New York Business 40 Under 40. In 2017, Vaynerchuk was listed as one of Forbes' Top Social Influencers
  • 2017 - in 2017, Vaynerchuk formed The Gallery, later renamed Gallery Media Group, a VaynerX subsidiary company that houses PureWow, male-oriented news outlet ONE37pm.com, and other media properties
  • 2017 - Vaynerchuk was a participant of Planet of the Apps, a reality television series, with will.i.am and Gwyneth Paltrow. In the show, Vaynerchuk and the team evaluated pitches from app developers vying for investment
  • 2019 - Vaynerchuk co-founded restaurant-reservation app, Resy, which was acquired by American Express in 2019. He is also a co-founder of winery Empathy Wines, which was acquired by Constellation Brands in 2020
